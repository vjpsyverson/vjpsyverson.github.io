Current research projects

My main project right now is at UC Merced, with a project on variation in small mammal paleocommunities from the late Quaternary of North America (NSF grant 2149416), with Jessica Blois, Marta Jarzyna, and André Bellvé. I'm currently assembling chronologies for a large number of fossil sites based on data in NeotomaDB, xDD, and other sources, after which André will model the constituent range distributions to understand community trait distributions.
