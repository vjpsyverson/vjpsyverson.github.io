Academic interests

I'm primarily a paleoecologist with a preference for Paleozoic invertebrates, especially crinoids.

My current projects

My main project right now is at UC Merced, with a project on variation in small mammal paleocommunities from the late Quaternary of North America(NSF grant 2149416), with Jessica Blois (website), Marta Jarzyna (website), and André Bellvé (website). I'm currently assembling chronologies for a large number of fossil sites based on data in NeotomaDB, xDD, and other sources, after which André will model the constituent range distributions to understand community trait distributions.
